Each run of _ becomes {n} of the chosen character class. Smart (default) matches anything THM doesn't explicitly reveal — dashes, colons, brackets, and other technical chars all fill underscore slots. Strict alnum tightens to letters/digits only. Any char removes all restrictions. Spaces, periods, and slashes in your mask are always matched literally as anchors.
